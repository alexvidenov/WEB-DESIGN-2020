About us

About us

Zecotec was established in Sofia in early 2010 with a clear idea and goal - to create products oriented to "green" environmental technologies. This is also evident from the name in the registration documents of the company. We are a small group of enthusiasts united around these ideas. Each of us has significant experience and experience in engineering fields such as application software, hardware, computer science, machine design and more.

What is our goal

Our incentive to work is the difficulties and challenges of new, fast-growing sectors, such as electric vehicles. Our aim is to provide our customers with quality, modern products, at affordable prices and with service and out-of-warranty support.
