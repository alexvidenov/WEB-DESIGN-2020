Free installation of a whole set!

When you buy a complete set of electric bicycles from us, which includes all the necessary components: (Battery, Controller, Display, Motor) to convert your bicycle into an electric one, you receive free installation and important instructions for proper operation and storage of the electric bicycle.

Warranty

Zecotec Motors service performs warranty and post-warranty repairs of all components included in the kits, ensuring the shortest possible time to eliminate the problem. We have in stock all the spare parts and the necessary equipment for this, and the people on our team are available to our customers every day during working and sometimes outside working hours.

Repair of the mechanical parts of the Bicycle

The service performs repair of all mechanical parts of your electric bicycle: Replacement of tires, Adjustment and repair of brakes, Adjustment and repair of Gears, Prevention and replacement of Chains, Centering of drops and many others. In the service we also repair all ordinary (non-electric) bicycles

New life for your old battery!

We offer complete diagnostics and repair of your old battery for an electric bicycle. We make a new package with new cells and electronics the size of the old box, keeping the mechanical construction in its original form. You get a battery with cells and electronics of the next generation and most often the bike performs better than the same as a new one. The new battery has a standard two-year warranty on our products.
