FAQ

What does each of our electric bike kits contain?
1. Brushless, three-phase DC (BLDC) motor for electric bicycle
The electric motors offered by Zekotek are manufactured in the factories in Suzhou - China. from the leading manufacturers in the industry: Bafang Electric Motor (8FUN) and XOFO motor. All motorcycle models are manufactured for us with pre-ordered and specified technical parameters and qualities according to our requirements. The range of engines includes those for "front" and "rear" drive, as well as a complete set for mounting the middle axle of the bike "mid drive motor".
2. Controller
In each electric vehicle, the engine is controlled by an electronic controller to which, in addition to it, all other electrical components are connected. The main functions of the controller are: To dose the energy from the battery to the motor according to our wishes and to perform the necessary types of protection in extreme situations.
3. LCD display for control and indication
This is the best display in its class! Technical parameters and main functionality:
- Operating voltage - 24, 36V, 48V (automatically recognized by the display)
- Digital, two-way communication with the controller.
- Screen lighting with adjustable brightness on 5 levels (backlight).
- Remote control buttons.
- Настройка на максималната мощност подавана към мотора.
- Вграден сензор за индикация на околната температура в гр.С.
- Показания за скорост – моментна, средна, максимална в км/ч.
4. Pedal sensor - PAS (pedal assistance sensor).
New design PAS sensor with integrated magnetic disk (12 x) for easy installation and increased operational reliability. Compared to standard sensors, the KT-V12 provides higher sensitivity and precision in adjustment. The output signal (12 pulses / vol.) Has a faster response when the power is turned on and automatically determines the direction of rotation.
5. Power regulators.
All controller models use a magnetic Hall effect sensor for control. They are not affected by water and do not wear out when used properly. They are manufactured by the leading company in the industry - Wuxing.
